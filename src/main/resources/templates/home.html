Welcome to the PMAS - Patient Medicine Appointment System

You can use this application to manage your appointments and medications.


Appointments
  • Leads to a page that lets you view, book or cancel your appointments with available doctors.

Medications
  • Leads to a page that lets you keep track of your medications.
  • You can view existing, add new ones or even remove unncecessary ones.

Profile
  • Leads to a page where you can view your profile.
  • You can view your personal details, emergency contact details and your medical history that you have shared with us.
